Blurp! Every 1st and 3rd Friday Curious Comedy has a improv battle royale!

A cage match show of two improv teams that will throwdown with mad improv skills to win the hearts and minds of the audience, who the audience chooses as the winner will live to (improv) fight again. This show is hosted by man-about-town, Jay Robert Flewelling. Visit Friday Night Fights on Facebook for more information. $7 online or $10 at the door.

Think your improv team has got the right stuff?

We're always looking for new teams to compete in Friday Night Fights! Every team has equal access and will be booked for a 17min. set on Thursday Night Throwdowns on a first come first serve basis. Winning TNT teams will be booked for Friday Night Fights.
